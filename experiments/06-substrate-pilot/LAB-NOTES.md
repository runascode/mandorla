# Lab Notes — Experiment 06 (Substrate Pilot)

A chronological, timestamped record of observations, surprises, and
parameter decisions made during Stage A (literature survey) and, on
proceed, Stage B (toy pilot). Distinguishes from the other documents
in this directory:

- **`PILOT-DESIGN.md`** — pre-PRECOMMIT design sketch for the two-stage
  program. `PRECOMMIT.md` for Stage B is locked only after Stage A
  produces the sharpened load-bearing claim (per
  [`STAGE-A-PLAN.md`](STAGE-A-PLAN.md) Task 5).
- **`STAGE-A-PLAN.md`** — implementation plan for Stage A: paper
  shortlists, deep reads, matrix fill, lineage prose, claim
  sharpening, Gate G1 decision.
- **`READING-LIST.md`** — paper shortlist by lineage, with tier and
  provisional axes-moved flags.
- **`READING-NOTES.md`** — per-paper deep-read notes by lineage,
  with definitive axes-moved flags and failure-mode entries.
- **`LAB-NOTES.md`** *(this file)* — *non-binding* observations.
  Reading progress, surprises, things-that-changed-the-shortlist,
  decisions made along the way. Future PRECOMMIT amendments cite
  entries here when they are triggered by what is observed.

Entries appended in chronological order; oldest at top.
